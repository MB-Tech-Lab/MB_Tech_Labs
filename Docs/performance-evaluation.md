# Performance Evaluation

## Purpose
The performance evaluation system measures individual and team productivity, quality, and adherence to commitments.

## Evaluation Levels

### Individual Level
Measures personal task execution, time management, and quality
- Evaluated by: Team Lead (primary), Manager (review)
- Frequency: Weekly and monthly summaries

### Team Level
Measures collective task completion and project progress
- Evaluated by: Team Lead, Then summarized by Manager
- Frequency: Weekly

### Manager Level
Measures team and project portfolio performance
- Evaluated by: Admin
- Frequency: Weekly and monthly

## Key Evaluation Metrics

### Task Completion Rate
- Percentage of assigned tasks completed on time
- Formula: Completed Tasks / Total Assigned Tasks
- Target: 90%+ for high performance

### Timeliness
- Meeting deadlines and submission schedules
- On-time count vs. late count
- Consistency across weeks
- Target: 95%+ on-time delivery

### Work Quality
- Submission review scores (Approved vs. Rejected)
- Rework required
- Client feedback and satisfaction
- Target: <5% rejection rate

### Consistency
- Regular daily work logging
- Consistent hours logged
- Minimal gaps or idle time
- Target: 8 hours logged per workday (or agreed part-time)

### Attendance Compliance
- Present during 9 AM – 5 PM work window
- Responsive and engaged
- Participation in meetings

## Performance Ratings

### Excellent (90-100%)
- Consistently meets or exceeds all metrics
- High-quality deliverables
- Shows initiative and problem-solving
- Reliable and dependable

### Good (75-89%)
- Meets most metrics consistently
- Acceptable quality with minor issues
- Generally reliable
- May have occasional gaps

### Satisfactory (60-74%)
- Meets minimum expectations
- Quality is acceptable with revisions
- Some inconsistency in performance
- May need improvement in specific areas

### Needs Improvement (<60%)
- Does not meet expectations
- Frequent quality issues
- Inconsistent work or time logging
- Requires intervention and coaching

## Stipend Eligibility

Stipend allocations (for interns) are based on performance ratings:

### Excellent Performance (90-100%)
- Full stipend eligibility
- Up to maximum stipend range
- Possible bonus for exceptional work

### Good Performance (75-89%)
- Full stipend eligibility
- Standard range

### Satisfactory Performance (60-74%)
- Partial stipend eligibility
- Reduced range
- Contingent on improvement

### Needs Improvement (<60%)
- Minimal or no stipend
- Requires performance improvement plan
- Stipend may be withheld until improvement

## Refund Eligibility

Refund eligibility (for program fees) is based on:
- Complete internship duration
- Satisfactory overall performance (60%+)
- No policy violations
- Acceptable attendance and time tracking

**Disqualifiers**:
- Fake or inflated time entries
- Prolonged inactivity (>3 consecutive days)
- Policy violations or misconduct
- Failure to complete program commitments

## Evaluation Process

### Daily Checks (Team Lead)
- Review daily submissions and time logs
- Flag issues immediately
- Provide same-day feedback

### Weekly Review (Team Lead & Manager)
- Compile task completion data
- Review quality metrics
- Calculate performance scores
- Identify trends or concerns
- Prepare weekly report

### Monthly Evaluation (Manager & Admin)
- Comprehensive performance review
- Multiple-month trend analysis
- Determine stipend/refund status
- Provide formal feedback
- Plan next month if continuing

## Feedback and Improvement
- Team Leads provide feedback on every submitted task
- Specific comments on quality and next steps
- Weekly summaries highlight patterns
- Monthly reviews include coaching and development suggestions
- Users can access feedback history anytime

## Appeals and Review
- Users can request review of evaluation decisions
- Manager reviews documented data
- Admin makes final appeal decision
- Disputes are resolved within 72 hours
