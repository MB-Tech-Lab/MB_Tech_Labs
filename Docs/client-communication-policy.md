# Client Communication Policy\n\n## Purpose\nThis policy defines who is authorized to communicate with clients on behalf of MB Tech Labs and establishes strict controls over external communication.\n\n## Authorization Hierarchy\n\n### Tier 1: Authorized (Default)\n- **Manager**: Full authority to communicate with clients\n- **Responsibilities**:\n  - Primary client contact point\n  - Gather and clarify requirements\n  - Provide project updates and status\n  - Handle client concerns and escalations\n  - Document all decisions and commitments\n  - Represent MB Tech Labs professionally\n\n### Tier 2: Authorized (By Exception)\n- **Team Lead**: Limited communication, only if explicitly authorized by Manager\n- **Conditions for Authorization**:\n  - Manager must explicitly approve Team Lead communication\n  - Authorization is documented in the system\n  - Communication is limited to specific topics (technical details only)\n  - Manager remains primary contact for business decisions\n  - All client interaction must be copied to Manager\n\n### Tier 3: Not Authorized\n- **Intern / Employee**: Strictly prohibited from direct client communication\n- **Interns/Employees must not**:\n  - Contact clients directly via email, phone, messaging, or any channel\n  - Respond to client inquiries without Manager approval\n  - Commit to timelines or deliverables\n  - Discuss pricing, scope, or business terms\n  - Share confidential or internal information\n  - Attend client meetings without Manager presence\n\n## Rationale\n- **Brand Protection**: All external communication represents MB Tech Labs\n- **Consistency**: Single point of contact ensures consistent messaging\n- **Compliance**: Legal and contractual commitments must come from authorized personnel\n- **Confidentiality**: Restricts sensitive information access\n- **Quality**: Prevents miscommunication or scope creep\n- **Accountability**: Clear responsibility chain for decisions\n\n## Prohibited Actions (All Roles)\n- Making commitments beyond task scope without approval\n- Sharing internal pricing, costs, or financial data\n- Disclosing other clients' information\n- Sharing MB Tech Labs' proprietary tools or methods\n- Making promises about deliverables not approved by Manager/Admin\n- Communicating during non-business hours without authorization\n- Using personal communication channels (personal email, social media, etc.)\n\n## Communication Channels\n- **Official Email**: Company email accounts only\n- **Project Management System**: System-based communication is tracked\n- **Scheduled Calls**: Calendar-tracked and documented\n- **Meetings**: Formal meetings with minutes and attendee records\n\n**Not Permitted**:\n- instant messaging (personal WhatsApp, Telegram, etc.)\n- personal social media\n- Untracked messaging apps\n- Verbal agreements without written confirmation\n\n## Client Requests Directed to Non-Authorized Staff\n- **If Intern/Employee receives direct client contact**: Immediately forward to Manager with context\n- **If Team Lead receives out-of-scope request**: Acknowledge and redirect to Manager\n- **No direct response** should be provided by unauthorized personnel\n- **Document** all incoming client contact attempts\n\n## Escalation Path\n```\nClient Contact\n  ↓\nIntern/Employee (if contacted) → Forward to Team Lead\n  ↓\nTeam Lead → Forward to Manager (unless pre-authorized)\n  ↓\nManager → Handles directly or escalates to Admin\n  ↓\nAdmin → Final authority on client decisions\n```\n\n## Compliance and Enforcement\n- **Violations** of client communication rules are serious policy breaches\n- **Consequences**:\n  - First violation: Written warning and retraining\n  - Subsequent violations: Suspension of system access or termination of engagement\n  - Serious violations (e.g., revealing confidential info): Immediate termination and legal action\n\n## Documentation Requirements\n- All client communication must be logged or archived\n- Email threads are automatically retained\n- Project management system records all discussions\n- Meeting minutes are required for significant client calls\n- Change requests and approvals are documented\n- Client agreements are stored centrally\n\n## Special Cases\n### Team Leads Attending Demos or Meetings\n- Team Lead may attend client meetings **if invited by Manager**\n- Manager remains lead communicator\n- Team Lead provides technical input only\n- No independent commitments or promises\n- All decisions go through Manager\n\n### Emergency Client Issues\n- If urgent client issue arises (production down, security breach, etc.):\n  - Team Lead can provide emergency technical details **only**\n  - Manager must be notified immediately\n  - No business decisions made without Manager\n  - All communication documented\n\n## Policy Review\nThis policy is reviewed annually and updated as needed to reflect organizational changes.\n
