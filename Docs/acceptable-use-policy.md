# Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

## Purpose
This policy defines acceptable behavior and prohibited actions within the MB Tech Labs Work Management System.

## Acceptable Behavior
Users must:
- Use the platform for assigned work and project tasks
- Log time accurately and honestly
- Submit real work and valid deliverables
- Communicate issues and delays transparently
- Respect rules for task status and reporting

## Prohibited Behavior

The following actions are strictly prohibited:

### Time Tracking Violations
- Creating fake or inflated time logs
- Timer manipulation or falsifying duration
- Logging time for unassigned tasks without approval
- Keeping timers active while not working
- Sharing timer/time log credentials with others
- Back-dating or modifying past entries

### System Misuse
- Attempting to bypass monitoring or tracking controls
- Accessing other users' tasks or reports without authorization
- Modifying task details or status inappropriately
- Deleting or corrupting audit trails
- Using system for personal projects

### Communication Violations
- Interns/Employees contacting clients directly
- Unauthorized Team Leads communicating with clients
- Disclosing client information to unauthorized parties
- Making commitments or promises on behalf of MB Tech Labs
- Sharing confidential project details externally

### Data and Security Violations
- Sharing login credentials
- Accessing systems without authorization
- Downloading confidential files for personal use
- Photographing or screenshotting confidential content
- Using unsecured or personal devices for company work

### Conduct Violations
- Fraudulent activity or intentional deception
- Harassment or abusive communication
- Discrimination or unprofessional behavior
- Insubordination or refusing reasonable directives
- Working while under influence of substances

## Enforcement

### Monitoring
- Admin and Managers review time logs and activity regularly
- Suspicious entries are flagged and investigated
- Pattern analysis identifies repeated violations
- Reports are generated for compliance review

### Consequences
Violations may result in:

**Minor Violations** (first occurrence):
- Verbal or written warning
- Required retraining
- Suspension of feature access (e.g., manual time entry)
- 72-hour suspension pending review

**Moderate Violations** (repeated or more serious):
- Written disciplinary notice
- Mandatory coaching or performance improvement plan
- Stipend or refund withholding
- Temporary system access suspension
- 1-week probation period

**Severe Violations** (fraud, major misconduct):
- Immediate termination of engagement
- Disqualification from stipend/refund
- Legal action and recovery of damages
- Reporting to authorities if applicable

## User Responsibilities

- Use the start/stop timer correctly for assigned work
- Keep work sessions aligned to actual task progress
- Record accurate notes for manual or exception entries
- Report any system issues or concerns promptly
- Comply with all policies and directives
- Seek clarification before taking uncertain actions

## Policy Alignment
This AUP is designed to support transparent, fair, and productive work for all team members. It complements MB Tech Labs' broader confidentiality, legal, and work monitoring policies.

## Appeal Process
- Users can request review of policy violation findings
- Appeals must be submitted within 5 business days
- Manager and Admin review documented evidence
- Final determination made within 10 business days
