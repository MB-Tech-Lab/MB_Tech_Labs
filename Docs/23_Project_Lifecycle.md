# MB Tech Labs - Project Lifecycle

## Overview

The Project Lifecycle document defines the end-to-end journey for projects at MB Tech Labs. It covers every stage from lead generation through client onboarding, delivery, and maintenance.

## Lifecycle Stages

### 1. Lead Generation
- **Public website** captures prospects through contact forms and product interest
- **Marketing campaigns** drive inbound leads
- **Lead scoring** evaluates opportunity fit
- **CRM integration** stores lead details for follow-up

### 2. Client Onboarding
- **Qualification call** to understand client needs
- **Client profile creation** in the system
- **Onboarding checklist** for project setup
- **Initial intake form** to capture project requirements

### 3. Requirement Gathering
- **Discovery workshops** with stakeholders
- **Requirement documentation** and user stories
- **Process mapping** for client workflows
- **Technical analysis** for architecture fit

### 4. Proposal & Quotation
- **Scope definition** and deliverables
- **Cost estimation** and pricing model
- **Milestone-based proposal** with payment terms
- **Client approval** and agreement signing

### 5. Project Creation
- **Project created** in MB Tech Labs system
- **Repository provisioned** in GitHub
- **Team members allocated** based on skills and availability
- **Project charter** and initial plan finalized

### 6. Team Assignment
- **Roles assigned**: project manager, team leader, developers, QA
- **Task breakdown** into features and milestones
- **Capacity planning** and workload balancing
- **Responsibilities confirmed** with each stakeholder

### 7. Development Phase
- **Requirements translated** into tasks and tickets
- **Feature development** on `feature/*` branches
- **Peer review** by senior developers and team leads
- **Staging deployment** for validation and testing
- **Regular progress updates** to client and stakeholders

### 8. Milestone Updates (Client Visible)
- **Milestone statuses** posted in client portal
- **Deliverable previews** shared with clients
- **Client approval flows** for completed milestones
- **Revision cycles** logged and managed

### 9. Testing & QA
- **Test plans** aligned to requirements
- **Functional testing** for deliverables
- **Regression testing** for system stability
- **Bug tracking** and resolution
- **Client acceptance testing** where applicable

### 10. Delivery
- **Final build** reviewed and approved
- **Production deployment** triggered from GitHub
- **Client acceptance** and sign-off
- **Handover documentation** provided
- **Training sessions** offered if needed

### 11. Feedback Collection
- **Client feedback survey** after delivery
- **Project retrospective** with delivery team
- **Issue capture** for follow-up improvements
- **Customer satisfaction metrics** recorded

### 12. Maintenance (Optional)
- **Support window** defined in contract
- **Maintenance plan** for ongoing updates
- **Bug fixes and enhancements** tracked in backlog
- **Customer success check-ins** scheduled

## Lifecycle Diagram

```
Lead generation -> Client onboarding -> Requirement gathering -> Proposal & quotation
-> Project creation -> Team assignment -> Development -> Milestones -> QA -> Delivery
-> Feedback -> Maintenance
```

## Process Ownership
- **Sales/Marketing**: Lead generation and client qualification
- **Project Management**: Onboarding, planning, and delivery
- **Product/Tech**: Requirement analysis and development execution
- **QA**: Testing and quality validation
- **Customer Success**: Feedback, handover, and maintenance

## Integration with MB Tech Labs Systems

### Public Website
- **Lead capture** from contact forms and product interest
- **SaaS product prospects** routed to onboarding workflows
- **Marketing analytics** linked to project intake

### Client Portal
- **Client onboarding data** stored in portal profile
- **Milestone updates** delivered through project views
- **Invoice and payment status** visible to clients
- **Feedback collection** integrated with portal reviews

### Employee Workspace
- **Team assignment** managed through workspace tools
- **Task tracking** provided to developers and team leads
- **Internal collaboration** coordinated through chat and meetings
- **Productivity tracking** aligns work with lifecycle progress

### GitHub Development Workflow
- **Project repositories** created at project initiation
- **Task and branch linkage** for development work
- **Code review and staging** aligned with lifecycle milestones
- **Deployment triggers** mapped to delivery stage

### Notification System
- **Lifecycle progress alerts** for stakeholders
- **Milestone completion notifications** for clients and teams
- **Approval requests** and status updates
- **Maintenance reminders** and support notifications

## Governance and Quality

### Stage Gates
- **Gate 1**: Lead validation and proposal approval
- **Gate 2**: Project setup and team allocation
- **Gate 3**: Development readiness and staging approval
- **Gate 4**: QA completion and client acceptance
- **Gate 5**: Final delivery and post-launch review

### Quality Controls
- **Documentation review** at every stage
- **Client sign-off** for scope and milestones
- **Risk assessment** during development and launch
- **Post-launch audit** and improvement plan

## Metrics and Success Criteria
- **Lead-to-project conversion rate**
- **Onboarding time** from contract to project creation
- **Milestone delivery accuracy**
- **Defect rate** during QA and post-launch
- **Client satisfaction score**
- **Maintenance response time**

## Continuous Improvement
- **Retrospectives** after each project
- **Process refinement** based on feedback
- **Knowledge sharing** across teams
- **Template updates** for future projects
- **Automation enhancements** for lifecycle tasks

The Project Lifecycle system guides MB Tech Labs through consistent, client-focused project delivery while supporting operational excellence and continuous improvement.
