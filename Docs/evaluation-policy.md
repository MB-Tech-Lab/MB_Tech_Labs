# Performance & Evaluation Policy

## Purpose
This policy defines how MB Tech Labs measures performance, determines active work, and applies stipend/refund criteria.

## Performance Measurements

Performance is evaluated using the following criteria:

### Task Completion Rate
- Assigned tasks completed ÷ total assigned tasks
- Target: 90%+ for Excellent, 75%+ for Good
- Measured weekly and monthly

### Timeliness
- Tasks submitted on or before deadline
- Consistency across multiple weeks
- Target: 95%+ on-time for Excellent
- Late submissions are tracked and weighted in evaluation

### Work Quality
- Task submissions approved on first review
- Rework required rate
- Client or Team Lead feedback score
- Target: <5% rejection rate for Excellent

### Consistency
- Regular daily work logging during 9 AM – 5 PM
- Stable hours logged per day
- Minimal gaps or idle time
- Target: 8 hours logged per workday (or agreed allocation)

### Attendance Compliance
- Present during standard work hours
- Responsive and engaged
- Participation in required meetings
- No unexplained absences

## Active Work Definition
Active work is defined as:
- A task with an active timer session and actual work being performed
- An assigned task with genuine progress toward completion
- An accurate task status update reflecting current work
- A time log entry tied to actual project work
- Meaningful progress updates or submission notes

**Not considered active work**:
- Timer running with no task progress
- Task assigned but no work started
- Time logged but task shows no progress
- Idle sessions longer than 15 minutes

## Stipend Eligibility

Stipend eligibility is based on performance criteria:

### Excellent (90-100% rating)
- Strong completion rate (90%+)
- On-time work (95%+)
- High-quality submissions (<5% rejection)
- Consistent time logging
- **Stipend**: Full eligibility, maximum range

### Good (75-89% rating)
- Acceptable completion (75%+)
- Mostly on-time (85%+)
- Quality acceptable, minor issues (<15% rejection)
- Generally consistent time logging
- **Stipend**: Full eligibility, standard range

### Satisfactory (60-74% rating)
- Minimum completion (60%+)
- On-time rate 70%+
- Quality acceptable with revisions (20% rejection)
- Inconsistent time logging
- **Stipend**: Partial eligibility, reduced range

### Below Satisfactory (<60% rating)
- Poor completion (<60%
)
- Frequent delays or missed deadlines
- Quality issues (>20% rejection)
- Poor time tracking compliance
- **Stipend**: Minimal or no eligibility

## Refund Eligibility

Refund eligibility is determined by policy compliance and commitment fulfillment.

### Eligible Conditions
Refunds may be approved if the user:
- Completes the agreed internship or engagement duration
- Maintains satisfactory performance (60%+ rating)
- Maintains valid work logs throughout engagement
- Avoids serious policy violations
- Demonstrates consistent attendance and productivity
- Submits required documentation and evaluations

### Disqualification Conditions
Refunds may be denied if the user:
- Submits fraudulent time entries
- Engages in excessive inactivity (>3 consecutive days)
- Misses multiple deadlines without valid cause
- Violates confidentiality or NDA
- Violates client communication policies
- Engages in misconduct or insubordination
- Fails to complete internship duration

## Evaluation Process

### Daily Level
- **Frequency**: Continuous
- **Actions**: Task submissions reviewed, time logs checked
- **Responsibility**: Team Lead

### Weekly Level  
- **Frequency**: Every 7 days
- **Actions**: Performance metrics compiled, rating calculated, report generated
- **Responsibility**: Team Lead and Manager

### Monthly Level
- **Frequency**: Every 30 days
- **Actions**: Comprehensive review, multi-month trends analyzed, stipend/refund decision made
- **Responsibility**: Manager and Admin

### Final Level
- **Frequency**: End of internship/engagement
- **Actions**: Complete performance summary, refund determination, completion report
- **Responsibility**: Admin

## Evaluation Transparency
- Users can access their own performance data
- Weekly performance summaries are provided
- Monthly ratings are explained in writing
- Feedback is specific and based on documented data
- Users can request clarification on evaluations

## Decision Authority
- **Daily/Weekly Decisions**: Team Lead
- **Monthly Stipend Decisions**: Manager
- **Refund Approval**: Admin
- **Appeals/Disputes**: Admin (final)

## Decisions are Based On
- Documented time logs (objective data)
- Task submissions and review outcomes (objective)
- Written feedback from Team Lead/Manager (subjective but documented)
- Performance trend analysis (multiple weeks of data)
- Policy compliance records (objective)

## Appeals and Review
- Users can request review of any evaluation decision
- Request must be submitted in writing within 5 business days
- Manager reviews all supporting documentation
- Admin makes final determination
- Decision provided within 10 business days
- Decisions are final after admin review

## Performance Improvement
- Users with Satisfactory or Below ratings receive coaching
- Specific improvement goals are defined
- Support and resources are provided
- Progress is reviewed weekly
- Improvement pathway to higher ratings is possible
